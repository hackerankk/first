Spiritual Tourism in Uttarakhand

1. Char Dham Yatra

The Char Dham Yatra includes visiting four sacred sites: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath.

2. Haridwar

One of the seven holiest places in Hinduism, Haridwar hosts the Kumbh Mela every 12 years.

3. Rishikesh

Rishikesh is known as the Yoga Capital of the World, attracting spiritual seekers and yoga enthusiasts.
