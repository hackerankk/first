Mussoorie - The Queen of Hills

About Mussoorie

Mussoorie, also known as 'The Queen of Hills', is one of the most popular hill stations in India. Located at a height of about 2,000 meters in the Garhwal region, Mussoorie offers stunning views of the Doon Valley and the surrounding snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas.

With its colonial charm, scenic landscapes, and cool climate, Mussoorie has been a favored destination for travelers looking for both relaxation and adventure.

Attractions in Mussoorie

  • Gun Hill: The second-highest point in Mussoorie, offering a panoramic view of the Himalayas and the Doon Valley.
  • Kempty Falls: A popular picnic spot with cascading waterfalls, located 15 km from Mussoorie.
  • Camel's Back Road: A 3 km long road shaped like a camel's back, perfect for leisurely walks and sunset views.
  • Lal Tibba: The highest point in Mussoorie, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks and valleys.
