Cuisine of Uttarakhand

1. Kafuli

A traditional dish made with spinach and fenugreek leaves, served with rice or roti.

2. Bhang Ki Chutney

This unique chutney is made from roasted hemp seeds, adding flavor to many meals.

3. Aloo Ke Gutke

A spicy potato dish often enjoyed as a snack or side dish, seasoned with local spices.
