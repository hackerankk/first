Wildlife Sanctuaries in Uttarakhand

1. Jim Corbett National Park

The oldest national park in India, home to a large population of Bengal tigers and other wildlife.

2. Rajaji National Park

This park is known for its elephants and leopards, and offers excellent wildlife viewing opportunities.

3. Nanda Devi Biosphere Reserve

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this reserve is renowned for its biodiversity and scenic beauty.
