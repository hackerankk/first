Rishikesh - Yoga Capital of the World

About Rishikesh

Rishikesh, located in the foothills of the Himalayas along the Ganges River, is renowned for its spiritual atmosphere and its status as the 'Yoga Capital of the World'. Pilgrims and tourists flock here for meditation, yoga retreats, and to experience the serenity of the sacred Ganges.

The city is also a gateway to the Char Dham Yatra, making it a prominent pilgrimage destination in India.

Attractions in Rishikesh

  • Lakshman Jhula: A famous suspension bridge over the Ganges offering stunning views of the river and temples.
  • Triveni Ghat: A sacred ghat where pilgrims perform the Ganga Aarti every evening.
  • Parmarth Niketan: One of the largest ashrams, offering yoga classes, spiritual lectures, and meditation retreats.
  • Rafting on the Ganges: Rishikesh is also an adventure hub, known for white-water rafting and other adventure sports.
