Nainital - The Lake District of India

About Nainital

Nainital is a picturesque hill station, nestled around the famous Naini Lake in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand. It is known for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, and charming colonial-era architecture. Surrounded by mountains, Nainital is an ideal retreat for nature lovers and travelers seeking a peaceful escape.

The town is named after the goddess Naina Devi, and the Naina Devi Temple located near the lake is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees.
