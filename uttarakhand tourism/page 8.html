Best Time to Visit Uttarakhand

Summer (March to June)

The summer season is ideal for visiting hill stations like Mussoorie, Nainital, and Auli. Itâ€™s a perfect time for sightseeing, trekking, and outdoor activities with pleasant weather.

Monsoon (July to September)

Monsoon brings heavy rainfall, making it a less favorable time for travel due to landslides. However, the Valley of Flowers is in full bloom, making it a spectacular destination during this time.

Winter (October to February)

Winter is perfect for snow lovers, especially in places like Auli, which is famous for skiing. The chilly weather is ideal for those seeking solitude in the mountains.
