Top Destinations in Uttarakhand

1. Mussoorie

Known as the "Queen of Hills," Mussoorie offers breathtaking views and is a favorite among tourists seeking a peaceful retreat.

2. Nainital

A beautiful hill station surrounded by lakes and mountains, Nainital is perfect for nature lovers.

3. Rishikesh and Haridwar

These cities are well known for their spiritual significance, attracting pilgrims from all over the world.
