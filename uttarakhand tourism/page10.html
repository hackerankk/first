2. Respect Local Culture

Many regions in Uttarakhand are deeply religious, especially in Haridwar and Rishikesh. Always respect local customs and traditions, especially around temples and holy sites.

3. Health Precautions

Ensure you are well-hydrated and acclimatize to higher altitudes when traveling to hill stations or trekking routes. Carry medications for altitude sickness if needed.

4. Safety Measures

During the monsoon season, be cautious of landslides and road blockages. Always check weather updates before planning treks or long journeys into the hills.
