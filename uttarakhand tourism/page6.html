Festivals of Uttarakhand

1. Kumbh Mela

A major pilgrimage and religious festival celebrated in Haridwar every 12 years.

2. Nanda Devi Raj Jat

An important festival honoring Goddess Nanda Devi, celebrated with a three-week pilgrimage.

3. Phool Dei

A harvest festival that marks the arrival of spring, celebrated with flower offerings and traditional songs.
