How to Reach Uttarakhand

By Air

The nearest airport is the Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, which is well connected to major Indian cities like Delhi and Mumbai. Pantnagar Airport also serves certain regions of Uttarakhand.

By Train

Uttarakhand is well connected by rail with major cities like Haridwar, Dehradun, Kathgodam, and Nainital. Haridwar and Dehradun serve as major railway junctions.

By Road

Uttarakhand is well connected to neighboring states by road. Buses and taxis operate regularly from cities like Delhi, Chandigarh, and Lucknow. The road network is extensive but challenging in hilly areas.
