Adventure Tourism in Uttarakhand

1. Trekking

Uttarakhand offers various trekking routes like Valley of Flowers, Roopkund, and Kedarkantha.

2. River Rafting

Rishikesh is a hub for river rafting, attracting adventure enthusiasts from across the world.

3. Camping

Camp under the stars amidst the stunning scenery of Uttarakhand, with popular spots like Chopta and Kanatal.
