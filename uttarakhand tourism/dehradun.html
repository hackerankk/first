Dehradun - Gateway to the Hills

About Dehradun

Dehradun, the capital city of Uttarakhand, is a charming blend of scenic beauty and historical significance. Nestled in the Doon Valley at the foothills of the Himalayas, it is known for its pleasant climate and proximity to popular hill stations like Mussoorie.

Dehradun is home to many renowned educational institutions, including the Forest Research Institute and the Indian Military Academy. The city offers a peaceful environment for those seeking a break from the hustle of big cities.

Attractions in Dehradun

  • Sahastradhara: Famous for its healing sulphur springs and stunning waterfalls.
  • Robber's Cave (Gucchu Pani): A river cave known for its natural beauty and adventurous terrain.
  • Mindrolling Monastery: A significant Tibetan Buddhist center with impressive architecture.
  • Tapkeshwar Temple: A popular Shiva temple nestled in a cave beside a river.
