Famous Destinations in Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is home to some of India’s most breathtaking destinations, each with its unique charm. From serene hill stations to bustling pilgrimage sites, every corner of this state has something special to offer. Here’s a quick guide to some of the most popular spots:

  • Dehradun - The state’s capital, known for its greenery, scenic hills, and historic buildings.
  • Nainital - A popular hill station with a central lake and picturesque mountain views.
  • Mussoorie - Called the ‘Queen of Hills,’ it offers mesmerizing views of the Doon Valley.
  • Rishikesh - Known as the Yoga Capital of the World and a hub for spiritual and adventure tourism.
  • Haridwar - One of the holiest cities in India, located along the Ganges River.
