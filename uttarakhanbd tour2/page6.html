Wildlife and National Parks

Uttarakhand is home to a rich diversity of flora and fauna, making it a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. The state boasts numerous national parks and wildlife sanctuaries, including Jim Corbett National Park, India’s oldest national park, where you can spot majestic tigers, elephants, and a variety of bird species.

Other popular wildlife spots include Rajaji National Park, where safaris take you through dense forests and allow you to witness animals like leopards, elephants, and deer. These parks offer unforgettable jungle experiences and encounters with the state’s remarkable biodiversity.
