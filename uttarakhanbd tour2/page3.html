Spiritual Significance

Uttarakhand is a spiritual sanctuary, attracting pilgrims from all over the world to its sacred sites. The state is home to the Char Dham - Kedarnath, Badrinath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri, which are considered the most important pilgrimage centers in Hinduism.

Beyond the Char Dham, Uttarakhand also offers many temples, ashrams, and holy rivers, providing a deeply spiritual experience for visitors. Haridwar and Rishikesh, two iconic cities, are renowned for their spiritual gatherings, including the Kumbh Mela, the largest religious gathering in the world.
